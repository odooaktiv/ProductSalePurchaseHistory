Product Sale & Purchase History

With this module user can easily track the history of product sold and purchase.

Prerequisites before Installation

No prerequisites are required before the installation.

Configurations

No configurations.

In the product form view you will find the Sale and Purchase history smart button.

On click of Sale History you will see the product sold history with all the sales details.

On click of Purchase History you will see the product purchase history with all the purchase details.
